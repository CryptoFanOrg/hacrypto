DIAC 2015

Drink coupon @ 1-Altitude

On Monday night (Sept. 28), registered attendees of DIAC 2015 are invited to the 1-Altitude in Singapore, located at the top floor of One Raffles Place.

Upon registration, participants will receive one coupon including one entrance to the 1-Altitude, as well as one drink to be redeemed at the bar.

Participants can choose to go anytime between 6:30pm and 12am on Monday 28 to redeem their drink.

Two tables are reserved for guests from DIAC 2015: B24 and B25.


Source: 1-altitude.com

Location

The 1-altitude (located at One Raffles Place) is a nice walk away from Novotel along the Singapore river.
