Sponsor Acknowledgments

The OpenSSL project depends on volunteer efforts and financial support from the end user community. That support comes in the form of donations and paid sponsorships, software support contracts, paid consulting services, and commissioned software development. Since all these activities support the continued development and improvement of OpenSSL we consider all these clients and customers as sponsors of the OpenSSL project.
